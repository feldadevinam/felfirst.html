✦ A Letter For Tengils! ✦
14 June 2026
Dear Tengils,

Hi guys... did u miss me? please say yes :D, jujur aja, I'm probably the one who randomly disappears, takes forever to reply, atau tiba-tiba muncul setelah sekian lama seolah gak terjadi apa-apa.

Gue gak selalu ada di setiap obrolan, gak selalu ikut semua momen, dan bahkan saat gue bikin ini gue gatau kalian lagi apa dan gimana keadaannya. But somehow, you guys always make me feel like I still belong here.

Even when I'm quiet, even when life gets busy and I disappear for a while, thank you for never making me feel left behind. Karena setiap kali gue balik, rasanya selalu kaya pulang ke tempat yang sama hangatnya.

Buat gue, Tengils means more to me than I usually show. Setiap tawa, setiap cerita random, setiap momen kecil yang kita bagi bareng selalu jadi bagian yang gue simpan baik-baik.

Please always save a little space for me. Wait for me sometimes. Miss me a little when I'm gone. And when I finally come back, welcome me like I've never left.

And no matter how busy life gets, I hope we'll always find our way back to each other.

Because no matter how far I wander, this will always feel like home. 🏡

With love & sparkles,
Estelle ✦
